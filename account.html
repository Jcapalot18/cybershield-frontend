Frequently asked questions
How is my security score calculated?
Your score starts at 50 and goes up or down based on trainings completed (+4 pts each), scans run (+15–20 pts), and breaches found (−3 pts each). Max is 100.
When does my trial end?
Your 14-day trial ends automatically. Go to the Billing tab to see your exact end date. You can cancel anytime before then with no charge.
How do I add my team?
Go to Dashboard → Team. Enter their name and email. They'll get a temporary password to sign in and run their own training and breach checks.
Is my data private?
Yes. We never sell your data. Email addresses sent to breach checking are only used to query HaveIBeenPwned — nothing is stored on their end. See our Privacy Policy.
