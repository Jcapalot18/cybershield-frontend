Refund Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026 · Effective: June 1, 2026
Our commitment: We want you to be completely satisfied with CyberShield. If you're not happy for any reason, contact us within 7 days of your first charge and we'll make it right.

1. Free Trial

All new accounts include a 14-day free trial. You will not be charged during the trial period. You can cancel at any time before the trial ends with no charge whatsoever.

2. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds in the following situations:

  • 7-day money-back guarantee — If you are charged and request a refund within 7 days of your first payment, we will issue a full refund, no questions asked.
  • Technical issues — If a verified technical issue on our end prevented you from using the service, we will refund the affected billing period.
  • Duplicate charges — If you were charged twice in error, we will immediately refund the duplicate charge.
Please note: Refunds are not available for partial months after the 7-day window, or if the account was suspended for violating our Terms of Service.

3. Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your Account Settings → Billing tab. When you cancel:

  • Your account remains active until the end of the current billing period
  • You will not be charged again after cancellation
  • No partial month refunds are issued after the 7-day window
  • Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation, then permanently deleted
